Welcome to Maharashtra

Explore the land of vibrant culture, stunning beaches, and ancient caves

       Monsoon is a magical time to travel in Maharashtra, from June to September. The rains turn the hills green and fill the rivers and waterfalls, making places like Lonavala, Mahabaleshwar, Malshej Ghat, and Bhandardara perfect for nature trips. It's a great season for trekking, road trips, and enjoying misty weather. While travel can be affected by heavy rain, the scenic beauty and refreshing climate make monsoon a favorite time for travelers in Maharashtra.
